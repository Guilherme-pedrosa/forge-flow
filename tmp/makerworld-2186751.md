### Description

**Hi, I’m Filip! 👋 Welcome to your safe printing harbor.**

With over **400+ models** in my library, I know exactly what you need: prints that just work. 🛡️ I run a **print farm**, so I test my designs in the real world, not just on a screen.

Whether you are printing from your **mobile app** 📱 or slicing on a PC, my goal is to give you a stress-free experience. No tinkering, no failed prints – just choose a profile and hit **Print** with confidence. ✅

**Explore the collection below and let’s make something awesome! 👇**

This is a **professional-grade rotating display** designed for serious market sellers and shop owners. It features a robust threaded system that allows you to fully adjust the height of each tier to fit keychains of any length. With just two tiers, this stand holds **over 140 keychains**!

**Key Features:**

- **Massive Capacity:** The two star-shaped holders alone can accommodate 140+ keychains.
- **Fully Adjustable Height:** The holders sit on a threaded rod and are secured with **lock wheels**. You can position them at any height you need – perfect for mixing long and short items.
- **Customizable Price Tags:** The top sign is designed to be a price tag (shown as "100" for 100 CZK in photos) or a category label. It is fully customizable to fit your needs.
- **Extra Stable Base:** The weighted base design ensures maximum stability even when fully loaded. It also doubles as a tray for loose prints or additional items.
- **Professional Equipment:** Designed specifically for high-volume display at craft fairs and expos.

**Assembly:** Simple assembly using the threaded rod and locking wheels. No glue required. The lock wheels ensure the tiers stay exactly where you set them.

**Feedback:** If you are interested in more accessories, different tier shapes, or specific modifications for your shop, **please let me know in the comments!**
