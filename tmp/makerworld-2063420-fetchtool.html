Modernly designed candle holder with elegant slats that create a beautiful shadow effect. The size is adapted for Ikea JAMLIK candles. With these candles, I haven't had any issues printing in PLA or PETG, lit for hours (Use candles at your own risk). I recommend PETG for safety as it withstands heat a bit better. You can also use LED candles.

 

For better printing, I have added small connectors between the slats to prevent the nozzle from moving them when depositing the filament. These can be easily removed once printing is finished. They are in a separate group within the object profile in case you prefer to print the candle holder without the connectors.
